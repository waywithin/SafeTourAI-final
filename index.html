Problem Statement

Tourists need safety intelligence before emergencies become serious

Tourist Safety Risks

  • Unfamiliar locations and routes
  • Local crime and scam-prone areas
  • Unsafe roads or isolated streets
  • Limited knowledge of nearby help centers

Emergency Challenges

  • Delayed response during panic situations
  • Language barriers in unknown cities
  • Internet unavailability while traveling
  • No trusted local emergency contacts

Solution Overview

A city-wise safety layer over regular maps

RT

Real-Time Monitoring

Tracks GPS position, route movement, risk indicators, and proximity to safe or unsafe zones.

SOS

SOS Emergency Alerts

Creates a location-rich emergency message and notifies trusted contacts through available channels.

AI

AI Risk Prediction

Scores the current situation using time, incident reports, crowd density, weather, and distance to help.

OFF

Offline Emergency Mode

Uses downloaded safety packs and GPS to warn users even when internet is unavailable.

Technical Architecture

A Feasible MVP Architecture

Frontend

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, later Flutter or React Native for mobile.

Backend

Flask or Node.js APIs for risk prediction, SOS requests, and help center search.

Database

PostgreSQL, MongoDB, or Firebase for users, locations, contacts, and safety zones.

AI Module

Random Forest Regression model, feature processing pipeline, safety score prediction engine, and risk classification layer.

Maps API

OpenStreetMap, Leaflet.js, Mapbox, or Google Maps for routes and zones.

Notifications

SMS gateway, push notifications, email alerts, and emergency dashboards.

Real-World Scenario

How SafeTour behaves during a risky travel moment

Tourist enters an unfamiliar crowded area at night

SafeTour detects a higher risk score because of time, low distance to a marked risk zone, reduced crowd safety, and past incident density. The app warns the tourist, suggests a safer route, highlights the nearest police station, and keeps SOS ready.

Detect increased risk Generate warning Suggest safer route Enable SOS Show nearest help center
